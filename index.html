Selective attention

Average response time: 1.2 sec
Correct responses:  80%

Tests your ability to perform a task in presence of a competing stimuli.

Congruent stimuli: Same text and color, for ex. GREEN
Incongruent stimuli: Different text and color, for ex. BLUE

sustained attention

Average response time: 0.8 sec
Correct responses: 60%

Tests your ability to keep yourself focused during a continous, repititive activity

Alternate attention

Average response time:  2.3 sec
Correct responses: 1 min 30 sec

Tests your ability to shift the focus of attention between tasks  having varied cognitive abilities.
